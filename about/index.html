Photographer Marte Johannessen Dæhlen

Marte Johannessen Dæhlen is a Norwegian photographer based in Oslo, Norway. She has finished several studies including, a 2 year study of photgraphy at Bilder Nordic: School of Photgraphy. She also has one year of pedagogy at the University of Oslo and a finished bachlors degree in specialised teacher training in Design, Arts and Crafts. The bachlor was taken at the Oslo and Akerhus University College.
