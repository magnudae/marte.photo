Photographer Marte Johannessen Dæhlen

Marte Johannessen Dæhlen is a Norwegian photographer based in Oslo, Norway. She has finished several studies including, a 2 year study of photography at Bilder Nordic: School of Photography. She also has one year of pedagogy at the University of Oslo and a finished bachelor's degree in specialised teacher training in Design, Arts and Crafts. The bachelor was taken at the Oslo and Akerhus University College.

Marte loves black and white photgraphy which is reflected in her work. She likes to focus on nature, the environment and people when capturing moments. This means that she often embark on long walks at new places for inspiration. Her camera is her most valuable possession when visiting both new and familiar places.
